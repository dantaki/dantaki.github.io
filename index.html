Marcus Aurelius Twitter Bot
This bot retrieves a user's tweets (say @realdonaldtrump), searches for words present in Marcus Aurelius' Meditations, and then helps the user to reply with a witty tweet swelling with wisdom.

I haven't really tweeted recently with this bot, but it probably one of the best examples of highbrow trolling.
