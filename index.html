Scientific Projects

Detecting structural variation in genomes sequenced with paired-end reads is extremely error prone.
I developed a machine learning model, SV2 that genotypes structural variation with a False Discovery Rate between 1-4%.

  • Publication in Bioinformatics
  • Repository

SV2 was the key method for the discovery of noncoding autism risk variants that were preferentially transmitted by fathers.
This work was published in Science

I dabble in competitive cooking show scrying using machine learning to predict the winner for the Great British Bake Off.
I posted my model, DeepBake on reddit in r/dataisbeautiful.

DeepBake is an ensemble of models for each week of competition, using features like rankings in technical challenges and star baker to predict who will win and who will leave the tent.

Marcus Aurelius Twitter Bot
This bot retrieves recent tweets from an account (say @realdonaldtrump), searches for words matching those in Marcus Aurelius' Meditations, and then helps the user to reply with a witty tweet swelling with wisdom.

I haven't really tweeted recently with this bot, but it probably one of the best examples of highbrow trolling.
